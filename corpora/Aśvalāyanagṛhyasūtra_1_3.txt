yat tu samānaṃ tad vakṣyāmaḥ // (2.1)
paścād agner dṛṣadam aśmānaṃ pratiṣṭhāpyottarapurastād udakumbhaṃ samanvārabdhāyāṃ hutvā tiṣṭhan pratyaṅmukhaḥ prāṅmukhyā āsīnāyā gṛbhṇāmi te saubhagatvāya hastam ity aṅguṣṭham eva gṛhṇīyād yadi kāmayīta pumāṃsa eva me putrā jāyerann iti // (3.1)

aryamaṇaṃ nu devaṃ kanyā agnim ayakṣata / (13.1)

varuṇaṃ nu devaṃ kanyā agnim ayakṣata / (13.3)
sa imāṃ devo varuṇaḥ preto muñcātu nāmutaḥ svāhā / (13.4)
pūṣaṇaṃ nu devaṃ kanyā agnim ayakṣata / (13.5)
sa imāṃ devaḥ pūṣā preto muñcātu nāmutaḥ svāhety avicchindaty añjaliṃ sruceva juhuyāt // (13.6)
apariṇīya śūrpapuṭenābhyātmaṃ tūṣṇīṃ caturtham // (14.1)

putrān vindāvahai bahūṃs te santu jaradaṣṭaya iti // (19.2)
